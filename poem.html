Title:An Overthinker(Freestyle Poetry)
I am good for a while
I'll talk more,laugh more
Sleep and eat normally
But then something happens
Like a switch turns off somewhere,
And all i am left with is the darkness of mind.
But each time it seems like I sink deeper and deeper......
And i am scared
Terrified that one day I won't make it backup
I feel like i am gasping for air screaming for help
but everyone just looks at me with confused faces
wondering what i am struggling just fine
And it makes me feel crazy
I sank deep into my thoughts
My thoughts taking me away from myself
Out of the depth of my thoughts ......
There is hope...
hope of.......
Hope of......what?
My heart is not responding
I.....I think it is still in the cage of darkness.....
