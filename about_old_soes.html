School of Engineering
Society

School of Engineering (SOE) is dedicated to promoting the STEM field, specifically Civil & computer Engineering. We work to provide resources for any student, alumni, and professionals regardless of an individual's degree of knowledge or commitment in the field of Engineering. SOES meetings and hosts events that provide opportunities for networking, educational, and professional development related to computer & Civil Engineering. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, resources, motivation, and training necessary to become successful leaders in STEM.

Join The Society

SOES is About

We aim to help every individual grow as a person and as as tech professional to feel confident in their tech skills once they graduate. On top of that, we are a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to network and succeed. Come join us at the SOES Club at Far western University.

Resources

Are you not sure where to start or where to look for resources? We compiled a bunch of resources that we believe are best suited to help you out in this endeavor. These resources include information on Computer engineering classes at Far western university, Data Science, Web Development, getting started on a new language, and much more.
