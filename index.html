Far Western University (FWU)
School of Engineering

The Far Western University (FWU) is offering the course in Bachelors Degree in Computer Engineering. The objective of this course is to produce skilled, qualified Computer Engineers that work for software development, network design and implementation, hardware and software troubleshooting, network security policies, etc. A strong theoretical foundation with sufficient practical exposure prepares students to solve real-world problems.

SALIENT FEATURES

Each course is specified with lecture, tutorial, and practical hours per week. The method of instruction is a lecture, enhanced by tutorials, and/or practical depending upon the relevancy of the course. Tutorials are used to widen the concepts stated in the course. Practical and laboratory classes are used to develop necessary concepts and basic skills.

Events

Welcome to our dynamic world of tech events! We, the students of computer engineering, are passionate about organizing a variety of engaging events that cater to the ever-changing realm of technology. From captivating graphics design workshops to immersive sessions on Git and GitHub, our events offer a platform for students to unleash their creativity, enhance their development skills, and stay ahead of the curve. With hackathons, coding competitions, and insightful tech talks also in our lineup, we foster a spirit of healthy competition, teamwork, and continuous learning. Join us on this exhilarating journey as we ignite your passion, connect you with like-minded individuals, and provide transformative experiences in the ever-thriving tech community. Stay tuned for updates on our upcoming events and get ready to be inspired, challenged, and amazed!

Collaborate

Our Computer Engineering department proudly collaborates with various esteemed IT organizations, including Leapfrog, Code for Change, and numerous other influential organizations in Nepal. These collaborations open up incredible opportunities for our students to engage in real-world projects, gain practical experience, and contribute to meaningful initiatives. Through these partnerships, we foster a strong industry-academia connection, enabling students to work alongside industry professionals, participate in hackathons, internships, and mentorship programs, and stay updated with the latest technological trends. These collaborations not only enrich our students' learning journey but also nurture a spirit of innovation, entrepreneurship, and social impact, empowering them to make a significant difference in the IT landscape of Nepal and beyond.
